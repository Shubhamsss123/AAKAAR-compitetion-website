Registration Form

LogiQ is an online quiz competition for civil engineering enthusiasts.

This competition will test your core knowledge based on your civil engineering syllabus.

The motto of this competition is to uplift the spirit of interested students and help them to go high in this field.

Participants must revise all their syllabi once for a better grasp of the question.

The competition has three phases:- phase1, phase2, and phase3.

Participants can give a quiz in any phase or all phases.

The best score among the three phases will be considered for the ranking.

Phase1: 25th November 2022, 10 AM-12 PM

Phase2: December, date and time will be announced after phase1.

Phase3: January, date and time will be announced after phase2.

Individual participants are only allowed.

The quiz portal link will reopen from 10 AM to 12 PM only on specified dates.

Malpractices will lead to disqualification of the registration.

The best score out of the three phases will be considered for the ranking.
